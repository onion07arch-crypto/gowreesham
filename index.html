About Gowreesham

Gowreesham is more than just a house — it's the heartbeat of our family. Nestled in the quiet village of Padavarad, Ollur, in the cultural capital of Kerala, Thrissur, our home stands as a testament to the warmth, love, and togetherness that defines us.

Named after Goddess Gowri (Parvathi), the embodiment of grace and prosperity, Gowreesham carries the blessings of tradition while embracing the joy of modern family life. Surrounded by lush greenery, swaying coconut palms, and the gentle sounds of nature, our home is a peaceful sanctuary where memories are made every day.

Located near the historic Orayampuram Ayyappankavu Bagavathy Temple, our neighbourhood is steeped in cultural richness and spiritual serenity. The famous Thrissur Pooram, Kerala's grandest temple festival, is just a stone's throw away — bringing colour, music, and celebration to our doorstep each year.
